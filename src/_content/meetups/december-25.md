17.30 – 19.30

Joy Ingle will be chatting through their recent commission ‘Alluvium’ for Delia Derbyshire Day, an arts charity based in Manchester dedicated to spreading the work of electronic music pioneer Delia Derbyshire and addressing inequalities in the field. Come along for a presentation on Derbyshire’s work, acousmatic storytelling, and the interplay of creative process with archives and heritage.
